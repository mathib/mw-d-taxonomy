Abstract

The MWV Damage ontology (MWV-D) is a taxonomy containing OWL classes to classify **construction damage**, extending [dot:ClassifiedDamage](https://w3id.org/dot#ClassifiedDamage). The taxonomy is manually created based on existing PDF reports of Monumentenwacht regarding damage inspections. In its current state, it contains content regarding timber damage [1] and damage caused by insects [2]. The main taxonomy is structured using RDFS subsumption axioms.

The namespace for MWV-D terms is https://w3id.org/con-tax/damages/mwv#

The suggested prefix for the MWV-D taxonomy is mwv-d

The Turtle version of the MWV-D taxonomy is available at https://w3id.org/con-tax/damages/mwv/mwv.ttl
The RDF/XML version of the MWV-D taxonomy is available at https://w3id.org/con-tax/damages/mwv/mwv.rdf
The JSON-LD version of the MWV-D taxonomy is available at https://w3id.org/con-tax/damages/mwv/mwv.jsonld
The N-Triples version of the MWV-D taxonomy is available at https://w3id.org/con-tax/damages/mwv/mwv.nt

The MWV-D taxonomy was created by Mathias Bonduel (KU Leuven) during his PhD research (2017-2020) funded by the Research Foundation Flanders in the form of a Strategic Basic Research grant (grant agreement 1S65917N).
